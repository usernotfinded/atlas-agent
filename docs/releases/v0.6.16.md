# Atlas Agent v0.6.16 Release Notes

> **Status:** current public GitHub release
> **Released:** 2026-06-30
> **Previous public release:** `v0.6.15`
> **Distribution:** GitHub-only; PyPI was not published
> **Not financial advice.** Atlas Agent is a software tool, not a financial
> advisor. Trading involves significant risk of loss.

## Summary

`v0.6.16` advances the bounded autonomy planning chain from CAND-001 through
CAND-008. The release adds deterministic, paper-only, local-first autonomous
workflow tooling, stateful paper-trading quality gates, read-only fixture-first
shadow-live comparison, and simulated-only governance rehearsals for gated submit
conformance, runtime readiness envelope evaluation, and operator approval gate
review.

All candidates remain evidence-only, simulated-only, and non-executing. No live
trading, live submit, broker order submission, provider execution, credential
loading, or order placement is enabled. The source/package version is `0.6.16`.
This is a GitHub-only release. PyPI was not published.

## Included Candidates

- CAND-001: Paper Autonomous Decision Loop and Shadow-Live Readiness Contract
- CAND-002: Autonomous Paper Decision Quality Scorecard and Promotion Gate
- CAND-003: Execution-Neutral Autonomous Trading Kernel and Stateful Paper Runner
- CAND-004: Autonomous Paper Trading Quality Gate
- CAND-005: Shadow-Live Read-Only Fixture-First Comparison
- CAND-006: Gated Submit Conformance Rehearsal — Simulated Only
- CAND-007: Runtime Readiness Envelope Evaluation — No Live Submit
- CAND-008: Operator Approval Gate and Kill-Switch Observation Fixture Review — Evidence Only

## Safety Boundaries

- The candidate chain remains paper-only, offline, and provider-free.
- Live trading remains disabled by default.
- Live submit remains disabled by default.
- Broker order submission is not enabled.
- Provider execution remains disabled by default.
- No real notifications are sent and no orders are generated or submitted.
- No real human approval is created or recorded.
- No credentials, secrets, account identifiers, or private financial data are
  included.
- `RiskManager`, approval gates, the kill switch, and audit hash-chain
  requirements are unchanged.
- No live-readiness, autonomous-live-readiness, trading safety, or permission to
  submit orders is claimed.
- CAND-006 remains simulated-only; CAND-007 remains no-live-submit; CAND-008
  remains evidence-only, simulated-only, and non-executing.
- No profitability, absolute-safety, or future-performance claim is made.
