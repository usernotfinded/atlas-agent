# Atlas Agent v0.5.7.dev6

## Summary

This is a development tag focused on adding a reproducible end-to-end demo for the paper-only research workflow.

- This is a **development tag**, not a production live-trading guarantee.
- No financial outcome is promised.
- Live trading remains disabled by default and explicitly gated.

## Highlights

- **Research workflow demo script** — `scripts/demo_research_workflow.sh` creates a temporary workspace and runs the full paper-only research chain: run -> list -> show -> plan -> verify -> summary.
- **Demo safety checks** — the script validates JSON outputs, artifact existence, workspace-relative paths, no pending orders, no absolute paths, and no secret-shaped strings.
- **Demo tests** — `tests/test_demo_research_workflow_script.py` provides 10 tests including structural checks, fake-atlas fixture tests, and safety-invariant validation.
- **Completed research chain** — run -> list/show -> plan -> verify -> summary, now with both CLI docs and a runnable demo script.

## Research Workflow

Commands:

- `atlas research run --symbol AAPL` — Creates a local paper-only research artifact.
- `atlas research list` — Read-only discovery of existing artifacts.
- `atlas research show RUN_ID` — Read-only inspection of a single artifact.
- `atlas research plan RUN_ID` — Creates a deterministic paper-only plan from a research artifact.
- `atlas research verify PLAN_ID` — Verifies a paper plan for completeness, paper-only constraints, and disallowed language. Creates a verification artifact.
- `atlas research summary` — Read-only overview of all research artifacts and paper plans.

Properties:

- Paper-only and analysis-only.
- Local artifacts only; no broker submit.
- No pending orders created.
- No approvals created.
- No live-trading authorization.

## Safety and Compatibility Notes

- Live submit remains disabled by default and gated.
- Research commands are not trading advice.
- Research commands do not generate live orders.
- Verification recommendation is for paper review only.
- Reconcile remains read-only and never calls `place_order` or `resolve_execution_broker("live")`.
- Safe quote gate remains execution-time only, not part of `BrokerResolver.can_submit`.
