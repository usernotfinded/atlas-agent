# Release v0.5.7.dev28 — Provider Opt-In Policy Artifact

## Summary

This release adds local provider opt-in policy artifacts and their integration into the research chain. No real provider execution is added. No network calls. No API keys. No provider SDKs. No broker changes.

Batch 8.1 introduces a deterministic, auditable policy artifact that can be created from a provider execution readiness report. It consolidates the opt-in stance into a standalone artifact with hash validation, replay, and timeline lineage. Provider execution remains disabled.

## What Changed

### New Artifact

- `src/atlas_agent/research/provider_opt_in_policy.py`
  - `build_provider_opt_in_policy_dict()`: deterministic policy artifact construction from a readiness report.
  - `create_provider_opt_in_policy()`: safe creation with validation, lineage, hash, and denylist scanning.
  - `safe_validate_provider_opt_in_policy_data()`: strict validation with invalid sentinel returns.
  - `validate_provider_opt_in_policy_artifact()`: detailed per-check validation.
  - `replay_provider_opt_in_policy()`: deterministic hash replay.
  - `iter_provider_opt_in_policy_artifacts()`: safe listing with invalid sentinels for tampered items.
  - `summarize_provider_opt_in_policy_for_run()`: read-only summary without writing artifacts.
  - Impossible boolean detection: `provider_opt_in_policy_impossible_boolean` for tampered flags.
  - denylist-clean manifest: stores only safe metadata; never stores raw forbidden fragments.

### New CLI Commands

- `atlas research provider-opt-in-policy READINESS_REPORT_ID`
  - Create a policy artifact from a readiness report.
- `atlas research provider-opt-in-policy-list`
  - List policy artifacts with safe sentinels.
- `atlas research provider-opt-in-policy-show POLICY_ID`
  - Show one policy artifact.
- `atlas research provider-opt-in-policy-validate POLICY_ID`
  - Validate a policy artifact with 10+ checks.
- `atlas research provider-opt-in-policy-replay POLICY_ID`
  - Replay and compare deterministic hashes.
- `atlas research provider-opt-in-policy-summary RUN_ID`
  - Read-only summary without writing artifacts.

All commands remain configless, local-only, and read-only where applicable.

### Integration

- `check-artifacts` now counts and validates provider opt-in policy artifacts.
- `timeline` now links policy artifacts under their source readiness report.
- `dossier` now includes policy artifacts in the research chain summary.
- Demo workflow extended with policy creation, validation, replay, summary, and timeline lineage checks.
