## Summary

`v0.6.14` closes the paper portfolio evidence chain from CAND-001 through
CAND-008. The release adds deterministic local documentation, demos, checkers,
tests, and reviewer evidence for paper portfolio proposals, stress constraints,
monitoring, rechecks, dossiers, replay, evidence closure, and final readiness.

The source/package version is `0.6.14`. This is a GitHub-only release. PyPI was
not published.

## Included Candidates

- CAND-001: Paper Portfolio Proposal Sandbox and Allocation Guard
- CAND-002: Paper Portfolio Stress Constraints and Drawdown Guard
- CAND-003: Paper Portfolio Monitoring Simulation and Recheck Gate
- CAND-004: Paper Portfolio Recheck Ledger and Human Review Queue
- CAND-005: Paper Portfolio Reviewer Dossier and Evidence Bundle
- CAND-006: Paper Portfolio Evidence Replay and Regression Gate
- CAND-007: Paper Portfolio Evidence Bundle and Candidate Closure Gate
- CAND-008: Final Paper Portfolio Release-Readiness Audit and Go/No-Go Dossier

## Safety Boundaries

- The candidate chain remains paper-only and offline/provider-free.
- Live trading remains disabled by default.
- Live submit remains disabled by default.
- Broker order submission is not enabled.
- Provider execution remains disabled by default.
- No real notifications are sent and no orders are generated or submitted.
- No credentials, secrets, account identifiers, or private financial data are
  included.
- `RiskManager`, approval gates, the kill switch, and audit hash-chain
  requirements are unchanged.
- No live-readiness or autonomous-live-readiness claim is made.
- Autonomous trading is not claimed.
- No profitability, absolute-safety, or future-performance claim is made.
