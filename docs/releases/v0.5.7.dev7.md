# Atlas Agent v0.5.7.dev7

## Summary

This is a development tag focused on paper-only research evaluation, end-to-end research demo integration, and command reference documentation.

- This is a **development tag**, not production live-trading readiness.
- No financial outcome is promised.
- Live trading remains disabled by default and explicitly gated.

## Highlights

- **Research evaluate command** — `atlas research evaluate PLAN_ID --data PATH` creates a deterministic evaluation artifact from a paper plan and local OHLCV data.
- **Evaluation artifacts** — local JSON artifacts with 9 deterministic checks: `plan_loaded`, `paper_only_mode`, `data_file_loaded`, `data_has_required_columns`, `data_has_rows`, `data_symbol_context`, `plan_has_verification_steps`, `plan_has_invalidation_checks`, `no_live_authorization_language`.
- **Evaluation metrics** — `row_count`, `first_date`, `last_date`, `latest_close`, `min_close`, `max_close`. No buy/sell recommendation, no signal, no expected profit.
- **Demo workflow integration** — `scripts/demo_research_workflow.sh` now covers the complete chain including evaluation.
- **Dedicated command reference** — `docs/research-workflow.md` documents all commands, artifact schemas, safety boundaries, and known limitations.
- **Full local research chain** — run -> list/show -> plan -> verify -> evaluate -> summary.

## Research Workflow

Commands:

- `atlas research run --symbol AAPL` — Creates a local paper-only research artifact.
- `atlas research list` — Read-only discovery of existing artifacts.
- `atlas research show RUN_ID` — Read-only inspection of a single artifact.
- `atlas research plan RUN_ID` — Creates a deterministic paper-only plan from a research artifact.
- `atlas research verify PLAN_ID` — Verifies a paper plan for completeness, paper-only constraints, and disallowed language. Creates a verification artifact.
- `atlas research evaluate PLAN_ID --data PATH` — Evaluates a paper plan against local OHLCV data. Creates an evaluation artifact.
- `atlas research summary` — Read-only overview of all research artifacts and paper plans.
- `./scripts/demo_research_workflow.sh` — End-to-end temporary-workspace demo of the full local chain.

Properties:

- Paper-only and analysis-only.
- Local artifacts only; no broker submit.
- Local CSV data for evaluation.
- No pending orders created.
- No approvals created.
- No live-trading authorization.

## Safety and Compatibility Notes

- Live submit remains disabled by default and gated.
- Research commands are not trading advice.
- Research commands do not generate live orders.
- Evaluation does not produce trading signals.
- Evaluation does not estimate profit.
- Verification/evaluation recommendations are for paper review only.
- Reconcile remains read-only and never calls `place_order` or `resolve_execution_broker("live")`.
- Safe quote gate remains execution-time only, not part of `BrokerResolver.can_submit`.
