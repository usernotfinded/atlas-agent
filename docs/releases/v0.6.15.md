# Atlas Agent v0.6.15 Release Notes

> **Status:** current public GitHub release
> **Released:** 2026-06-22
> **Previous public release:** `v0.6.14`
> **Distribution:** GitHub-only; PyPI was not published
> **Not financial advice.** Atlas Agent is a software tool, not a financial
> advisor. Trading involves significant risk of loss.

## Summary

`v0.6.15` closes the paper human review chain from CAND-001 through CAND-006.
The release adds deterministic local documentation, demos, checkers, tests, and
reviewer evidence for paper human review packs, review ledgers, policy
simulation, replay regression gates, evidence bundles, and final release
readiness audit.

The source/package version is `0.6.15`. This is a GitHub-only release. PyPI was
not published.

## Included Candidates

- CAND-001: Paper Human Review Pack and Non-Executable Suggestion Gate
- CAND-002: Paper Human Review Decision Ledger and Approval Gate
- CAND-003: Paper Human Review Policy Simulator and Blocked-Live Gate
- CAND-004: Paper Human Review Replay and Regression Gate
- CAND-005: Paper Human Review Evidence Bundle and Candidate Closure Gate
- CAND-006: Final Human Review Release-Readiness Audit and Go/No-Go Dossier

## Safety Boundaries

- The candidate chain remains paper-only and offline/provider-free.
- Live trading remains disabled by default.
- Live submit remains disabled by default.
- Broker order submission is not enabled.
- Provider execution remains disabled by default.
- No real notifications are sent and no orders are generated or submitted.
- No real human approval is created or recorded.
- No credentials, secrets, account identifiers, or private financial data are
  included.
- `RiskManager`, approval gates, the kill switch, and audit hash-chain
  requirements are unchanged.
- No live-readiness or autonomous-live-readiness claim is made.
- Autonomous trading is not claimed.
- No profitability, absolute-safety, or future-performance claim is made.
