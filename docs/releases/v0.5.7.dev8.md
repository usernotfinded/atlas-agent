# Atlas Agent v0.5.7.dev8

## Summary

This is a development tag focused on research artifact schema versioning and read-only artifact health checking.

- This is a **development tag**, not production live-trading readiness.
- No financial outcome is promised.
- Live trading remains disabled by default and explicitly gated.

## Highlights

- **Research artifact schema versioning** — all research workflow artifacts now include `schema_version: "1"`.
- **Legacy artifact compatibility** — artifacts without `schema_version` continue to load where safe.
- **Fail-closed unsupported schema handling** — unsupported future schema versions fail closed for commands that load full artifacts (`show`, `plan`, `verify`, `evaluate`). `list` and `summary` skip them safely.
- **Research artifact health check** — `atlas research check-artifacts` performs a read-only scan of local research artifacts.
- **Demo integration** — `scripts/demo_research_workflow.sh` now validates artifact health at the end of the chain.

## Research Artifact Health

Commands:

- `atlas research check-artifacts` — Read-only health check of local research artifacts.
- `atlas research check-artifacts --json` — JSON output with counts, issues, and warnings.
- `atlas research check-artifacts --strict` — Exits with code `2` when issues are found.

Detects:

- Malformed JSON
- Unsupported schema versions
- Legacy artifacts missing `schema_version`
- Duplicate IDs
- Missing required fields
- Symbol mismatches
- Unsafe paths and symlink escapes outside the workspace
- Unexpected artifact locations

Properties:

- Read-only; never modifies artifacts.
- No migration, rewrite, repair, or deletion of artifacts.
- Does not create pending orders or approvals.

## Research Workflow

Commands:

- `atlas research run --symbol AAPL` — Creates a local paper-only research artifact.
- `atlas research list` — Read-only discovery of existing artifacts.
- `atlas research show RUN_ID` — Read-only inspection of a single artifact.
- `atlas research plan RUN_ID` — Creates a deterministic paper-only plan from a research artifact.
- `atlas research verify PLAN_ID` — Verifies a paper plan for completeness, paper-only constraints, and disallowed language. Creates a verification artifact.
- `atlas research evaluate PLAN_ID --data PATH` — Evaluates a paper plan against local OHLCV data. Creates an evaluation artifact.
- `atlas research summary` — Read-only overview of all research artifacts and paper plans.
- `atlas research check-artifacts` — Read-only health check of local research artifacts.
- `./scripts/demo_research_workflow.sh` — End-to-end temporary-workspace demo of the full local chain.

Properties:

- Paper-only and analysis-only.
- Local artifacts only; no broker submit.
- No pending orders created.
- No approvals created.
- No live-trading authorization.

## Safety and Compatibility Notes

- Live submit remains disabled by default and gated.
- Research commands are not trading advice.
- Evaluation does not produce trading signals.
- Evaluation does not estimate profit.
- Research commands do not generate live orders.
- `check-artifacts` is a local inspection command only.
- Reconcile remains read-only.
- Safe quote gate remains execution-time only.
