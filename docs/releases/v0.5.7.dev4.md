# Atlas Agent v0.5.7.dev4

## Summary

This is a development tag focused on research workflow, safe quote gate, workspace hygiene, and docs-truth hardening.

- This is a **development tag**, not a production live-trading guarantee.
- No financial outcome is promised.
- Live trading remains disabled by default and explicitly gated.

## Highlights

- **Safe quote gate for market-order risk revalidation** — execution-time gate with conservative pricing (ask for buy, bid for sell) and freshness checking. Market orders remain blocked by default when no quote is supplied.
- **Research workflow** — `atlas research run/list/show/plan/summary` commands for paper-only, analysis-only research.
- **Research artifacts and paper plan artifacts** — local JSON artifacts with stable schemas, workspace-relative paths, and safe event metadata.
- **Workspace hygiene guard** — `scripts/check_no_protected_staged.py` blocks protected local/runtime artifacts from being staged.
- **Docs-truth tests** for quote gate and research workflow protect README.md and docs/architecture.md from drift.

## Research Workflow

Commands:

- `atlas research run --symbol AAPL` — Creates a local paper-only research artifact.
- `atlas research list` — Read-only discovery of existing artifacts.
- `atlas research show RUN_ID` — Read-only inspection of a single artifact.
- `atlas research plan RUN_ID` — Creates a deterministic paper-only plan from a research artifact.
- `atlas research summary` — Read-only overview of all research artifacts and paper plans.

Properties:

- Paper-only and analysis-only.
- Local artifacts only; no broker submit.
- No pending orders created.
- No approvals created.
- No live-trading authorization.

## Safety and Compatibility Notes

- Live submit remains disabled by default and gated.
- Quote gate does not make market orders safe; it is an execution-time revalidation layer.
- Missing, stale, or malformed quotes block market-order submit execution.
- Buy uses ask, sell uses bid for risk revalidation.
- Quote gate is not part of `BrokerResolver.can_submit`.
- Reconcile remains read-only and never calls `place_order` or `resolve_execution_broker("live")`.
- Research commands are not trading advice.
